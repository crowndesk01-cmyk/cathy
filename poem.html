Ode to Cathy

Certified by the International Society of People Who Are Extremely Fond of You.

Roses are red,
Violets are blue,
I Googled "perfect person"
and it showed me you.

I burnt my toast again today,
forgot I had a pan,
I was too busy daydreaming
about being your man.

You laugh and I forget
what gravity is for,
you smile and honestly Cathy
my knees hit the floor.

Some people write love songs,
some write novels or art —
I built a website for you,
straight from my heart.

So here is my poem,
my webpage, my proof —
I love you completely,
you absolute goof. 💖

✨🌸✨

玫瑰係紅色，
紫羅蘭係藍色，
我Google「完美嘅人」
結果出現咗你。

我今日又焗焦咗多士，
唔記得鑊仲喺爐上，
因為我一直發緊夢
諗住點先可以娶你返屋企。

你一笑，我唔記得
地心吸力係乜嘢，
你微笑，老實講Cathy
我雙腳軟晒。

有人寫情歌，
有人寫小說或畫畫 —
我幫你建咗個網頁，
直接由心而發。

所以呢首係我嘅詩，
我嘅網頁，我嘅証明 —
我完全愛你，
你呢個傻瓜。 💖
