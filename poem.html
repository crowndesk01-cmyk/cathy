Ode to Cathy

Certified by the International Society of People Helplessly Fond of You.

Roses are red,
violets are blue,
I Googled "perfect person"
and it just showed me you.

I burnt the pasta again today —
stood there watching it smoke,
too busy thinking about
your laugh, your face,
and how completely gone I am for you.

You smile and gravity
forgets its whole job.
You text first and I have to put
the phone down and walk
around the room exactly twice. 🫠

You held me on the hard days.
You stayed through the messy parts.
You showed up — quietly, steadily,
like you always knew I'd need you there.
(I always did.)

Some people write songs,
some paint, some buy flowers —
I built a whole website, you dummy,
straight from my embarrassingly big heart. 💖

✨🌸✨

玫瑰係紅色，
紫羅蘭係藍色，
我Google「最完美嘅人」，
佢就show咗你出嚟。

我今日又煮燶咗意粉——
企喺度睇住佢冒煙，
淨係諗住你嘅笑聲、你嘅樣，
同我有幾完全為你而瘋。

你一笑，地心吸力
突然唔記得做嘢。
你先send訊息，我要放低電話
喺房間行足兩圈先平靜返。🫠

喺我最難熬嘅日子，你撐住我。
你喺一片混亂中留低。
你出現——安靜地、堅定地，
好似你一早知道我需要你。
（我係需要。）

有人寫情歌，
有人畫畫、有人買花——
我整咗成個網站俾你，蠢蛋，
由我個大得出奇嘅蠢心而發。💖

Be honest... did you feel something? 🥹
