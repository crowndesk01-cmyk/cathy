A Letter For You, Cathy

My dearest Cathy,

I built this entire website because I am slightly dramatic and very much in love with you. Every page, every joke, every terrible pun — it was all just an excuse to tell you how I feel.

You make every ordinary day feel like something worth writing about. I want to be there for every single one of them — the good days, the hard days, and every random Tuesday in between.

You have supported me through more than I can say. Every time I needed someone, you were there. I promise to be that person for you — always, without question.

I'm sorry for every mistake. I'm still learning, and I'll keep learning — for you. We will grow together, travel together, argue about blankets together, and love each other through all of it.

✨ 💍 ✨

我最親愛嘅Cathy，

我建立呢個整個網站係因為我有少少戲劇化同埋非常愛你。

你令每一個平凡嘅日子都感覺值得記錄。我想陪伴你每一日。

你支持咗我好多。我承諾會做你嘅那個人 — 永遠。

對唔住我嘅過失。我哋會一齊成長，一齊旅行，永遠相愛。
