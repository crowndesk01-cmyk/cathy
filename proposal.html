Will you be my future wife, Cathy?

My dearest Cathy,

I built this entire website because I am slightly dramatic and very much in love with you. Every page, every joke, every terrible pun — it was all just an excuse to tell you how I feel.

You make every ordinary day feel like something worth writing about. And I want a lifetime of ordinary days with you.

So, Cathy — will you be my person? Officially. With a ring and everything. (And joint custody of all the snacks.)

✨ 💍 ✨

所以，Cathy — 你願意做我嘅人嗎？正式地。有戒指嗰種。（同埋所有零食嘅共同監護權。）
