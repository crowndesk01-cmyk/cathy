A Letter for You

My dearest Cathy,

I built this entire website because I am, at my core, a little dramatic — and a whole lot in love with you.

Every chapter, every terrible pun, every exhibit and article and poem — it was all just a very elaborate way of saying something I mean more than any of it: I see you. I choose you. I want to keep seeing you and choosing you for as long as you'll have me.

You were there on the hard days. You showed up quietly, steadily, when I needed it most. You held space for the messy parts of me without making me feel like a burden. You stayed.

I'm sorry for the times I fell short. For the moments I went quiet when I should have spoken. For the times I let you carry things you shouldn't have had to carry alone. I'm still learning. I will keep learning.

But here's what I know for certain: I want to be there for you the way you've been there for me. On the bad days and the good ones. Through the career goals and the 3am doubts and the airports and the ordinary Tuesdays and every complicated, beautiful, messy thing in between.

I want to travel the world with you — from here to wherever the next chapter takes us. I want to build something with you. I want to grow with you.

You are the person I want standing next to me for all of it.

So — there's one more chapter. And one more question.

— Shreyas 💍

我最親愛嘅Cathy，

我整咗成個網站，因為我骨子裡有少少戲劇化——同非常非常愛你。

每一章、每個爛諧音梗、每件證物同每條合約條款同每首詩——全部都係一個非常複雜嘅方式去講一件我比任何嘢都更認真嘅事：我看見你。我選擇你。只要你願意，我想永遠咁看見你、選擇你。

你喺我最難熬嘅日子出現。你靜靜地、穩定地出現，在我最需要你嘅時候。你包容咗我亂糟糟嘅一面，冇令我覺得係負擔。你留低咗。

對唔住我有時未做到。對唔住我本應開口時選擇沉默。對唔住有時讓你承受了不應該獨自承受嘅嘢。我仲喺學習中。我會繼續學習。

但有一件事我非常確定：我想以你陪伴我嘅方式陪伴你。 喺壞日子同好日子。喺事業目標同凌晨3點嘅懷疑同機場同平凡嘅星期二同每一件複雜、美麗、混亂嘅事之中。

我想同你一齊環遊世界——由呢度去到下一章帶我哋去嘅任何地方。我想同你一齊建立嘢。我想同你一齊成長。

你係我想要站在我身旁嘅那個人。

所以——仲有最後一章。同最後一個問題。

— Shreyas 💍
